About Me

Greetings! I'm Justin DeMatteis.

Welcome to my world, where the tapestry of life unfolds in a unique blend of Aries energy and the vibrancy of the Year of the Dragon. Let me share with you the intricacies of my journey and the joys that define my existence.

Family and Furry Companions

Nestled in the comforting embrace of marriage, I am the proud parent of a boy and a girl, a combination that has earned us the endearing title of the "million-dollar family." Their love and joy are the pillars of my gratitude.

In our cozy abode, the picture is completed by two furry companions – a seasoned 12-year-old Shih Tzu and a spirited 4-year-old American Doberman. Their presence adds an extra layer of warmth and laughter to our daily adventures.

The Journey of Life Begins

Embarking on the journey of life, my narrative unfolds through a captivating tapestry woven with the threads of family bonds and the delightful chaos of parenthood. Each lesson learned, shared laughter, and conquered challenge has sculpted the person I am today. Life, in its artistic brilliance, has meticulously crafted a tale that I'm eager to share with you.

Professional Path and Pivotal Moments

The prologue to my journey begins in the fascinating world of precision and engineering. In the roles of a Tool and Die Designer and CNC Programmer Machinist, the rhythmic hum of machinery and the intricate dance of design were my daily companions.

Yet, as life's chapters unfolded, an unexpected twist altered the trajectory of my career. A poignant event resulted in a permanent injury, compelling me to reassess my path and make pivotal decisions about the future. In the face of adversity, I discovered resilience and a newfound determination to explore uncharted territories.

Passions Beyond Roles

Beyond the roles of a devoted partner and a loving parent, I find joy in watching a Movie with my wife or spending the hot summer days lying around the pool with the family. These moments of passion provide balance and serve as a constant source of inspiration amid the whirlwind of everyday life.

Guided by Gratitude

Gratitude is the compass guiding my journey. Whether savoring the simple pleasures of a shared meal, relishing the symphony of children's laughter, or basking in the comforting presence of loyal pets, I believe in cherishing each moment and expressing gratitude for the inherent beauty it brings.

Join Me on this Journey

This space is a canvas where I paint reflections of my experiences – the joys, challenges, and musings of this remarkable journey. I warmly invite you to join me, Justin DeMatteis, as I navigate the chapters of life, embracing its unpredictability with an open heart and a spirit full of gratitude.

Thank you for peering into my world. I'm genuinely thrilled to connect and share more with you as we traverse the ever-evolving tapestry of life together. I gear to form new relationships and collaborate on any future endeavors.

Warm regards,
